A pivot point in alternative music. The record explores alienation and connection in an era of accelerating change.

## Track notes (highlights)
- Airbag — opener that fuses breakbeat-inspired drum edits with guitar surge; title nods to the safety mechanism.
- Paranoid Android — multi-part suite; live versions stretched dynamics even further.
- Exit Music (For a Film) — written for a closing scene; tape-saturated, intimate, then overwhelming.
- Let Down — interlocking guitar arpeggios and stacked vocal lines; recorded live to capture feel.

### Fun facts
- Working titles and cryptic interludes in the artwork hinted at an off-kilter operating system.
- Fans tracked hidden station idents and snippets across singles and tour visuals.

### Notable live moments
- The 1997–98 tour saw expansive versions of Paranoid Android and Exit Music.
- Early versions of Lift and Polyethylene appeared in setlists.
